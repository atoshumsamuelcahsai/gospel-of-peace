Who We Are

Gospel of Peace Church is a vibrant Christian community located in the heart of Glasgow, Scotland. We are a welcoming congregation dedicated to worship, fellowship, and serving our local community with love and grace.

Our Mission: To create a welcoming space where people can discover God's love, grow in faith, and serve others with compassion and joy.

Our Story

Founded over 15 years ago, Gospel of Peace Church has been a cornerstone of faith in Glasgow. What started as a small gathering of believers has grown into a thriving community of over 100 active members.

Our journey has been marked by:

  • Authentic worship and powerful teaching
  • Genuine community and lasting friendships
  • Service to our local Glasgow community
  • Outreach programs and social justice initiatives
  • Youth and family ministries

What We Believe

We are a Christian church that believes in:

  • The Bible as God's inspired word and our guide for life
  • Jesus Christ as our Lord and Savior
  • Grace and Love as the foundation of our faith
  • Community as essential for spiritual growth
  • Service as our calling to love others
